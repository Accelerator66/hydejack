Solid Yellow

Projects* | 2017 | Links:

This flavor of Hydejack changes the accent color and sidebar color to the same yellow as the “0a”-theme of the original Hyde Jekyll theme.

To use this flavor, change your _config.yml to include:

accent_color: '#f4bf75'
accent_image: none
